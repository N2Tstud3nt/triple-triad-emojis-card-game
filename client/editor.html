Build A Deck (24/125)
Instructions
⭐ Hero - Increase maximal cost by 7.
🔥 Underdog - Captures attacked tied sides if they have a higher card cost.
⚔️ Tiebreaker - If 2 or more attacked sides are tied with this, capture them.
💰 Bribe - If 2 or more attacked sides have equal cost as this, capture them.
☘️ Ambush - If my cost equals the sum of all attacked sides, capture them.
👋 Reach - Attacks cards over the adjacent allied cards.
⛓️ Chain - If I'm captured or capture with an ability, replay captured card(s).
🧛 Veil - Hide the sides and abilities of this card until it captures or is captured.
📌 Spike - Deal a normal attack to cards normally played next to this card.

Gameplay
Two players alternate placing cards on a 3×3 grid. The decks are public (except 🧛 cards). Played card attacks adjacent cards of opposite loyalty. Attacked cards with weaker sides are captured and change loyalty. Game ends on full grid, and the player with most captures wins.
